FRAMES

Check out some of of our available frames

From sleek and modern to classic and timeless, our diverse range of frames offers something for everyone. Designed with both style and comfort in mind, you're sure to find the perfect fit for your unique look and lifestyle.

Full Rim Frames: These frames completely encircle the lenses and are the most common type of eyeglass frame.

Semi-Rimless Frames: These frames have a frame on the top part of the lenses, while the bottom part is left rimless..

Rimless Frames: These frames don't have any rim around the lenses and are held in place by screws or a nylon cord.

Cat Eye Frames: These frames have an upswept shape on the upper outer edges of the frames, resembling a cat's eye.

Round Frames: These frames are circular in shape and were popularized in the 1960s.

Aviator Frames: These frames are originally designed for pilots and have a teardrop shape with thin metal frames.

Butterfly Frames: These frames are larger and more oversized than most frames, with a shape that resembles a butterfly's wings.
